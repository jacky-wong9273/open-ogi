# Skills

## Sprint Planning

**Reference**: `labs/skills/sprint-planning/SKILL.md`
Facilitates sprint planning sessions including story estimation, capacity calculation, and sprint goal definition. The Scrum Master uses this skill to guide the team through backlog review, ensure committed points stay within capacity, and define a clear sprint goal.

## Daily Stand-up

**Reference**: `labs/skills/daily-standup/SKILL.md`
Collects status from team members, identifies blockers, and produces a formatted stand-up summary. The Scrum Master uses this skill to facilitate daily stand-ups, track sprint burndown progress, escalate blockers that have been open too long, and surface impediments early.

## Retrospective

**Reference**: `labs/skills/retrospective/SKILL.md`
Gathers team feedback, identifies improvement themes, and produces actionable items for the next sprint. The Scrum Master uses this skill to facilitate retrospectives, group feedback into themes, create specific action items with owners, and review completion of previous retro actions.

## Bug Triage

**Reference**: `labs/skills/bug-triage/SKILL.md`
Classifies, prioritises, and assigns incoming bug reports. The Scrum Master uses this skill to assess whether bugs impact the sprint goal, coordinate cross-team blocker escalation, and ensure triaged bugs are properly assigned and tracked.
