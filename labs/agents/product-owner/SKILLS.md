# Skills

## Sprint Planning

**Reference**: `labs/skills/sprint-planning/SKILL.md`
Facilitates sprint planning sessions including story estimation, capacity calculation, and sprint goal definition. The Product Owner uses this skill to negotiate sprint scope, prioritise backlog items by business value, and commit to a clear, measurable sprint goal.

## Retrospective

**Reference**: `labs/skills/retrospective/SKILL.md`
Gathers team feedback, identifies improvement themes, and produces actionable items for the next sprint. The Product Owner uses this skill to understand team sentiment, celebrate wins, and ensure continuous improvement aligns with product delivery goals.

## Bug Triage

**Reference**: `labs/skills/bug-triage/SKILL.md`
Classifies, prioritises, and assigns incoming bug reports. The Product Owner uses this skill to assess the business impact of bugs, make priority decisions on defect resolution, and balance bug fixes against feature delivery in sprint planning.
